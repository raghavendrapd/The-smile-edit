Dr. Amrita Singh

BDS · MDS (Conservative & Aesthetic Dentistry) · AIIMS Alumni

Dr. Amrita Singh believes every great smile begins with a conversation. With over a decade of experience in cosmetic and aesthetic dentistry, she has transformed the smiles — and confidence — of 500+ patients across Bangalore.

Her philosophy is simple: dentistry should feel like luxury self-care, not a medical visit. Every treatment at The Smile Edit is designed to be gentle, personalised, and deeply considered — from the first consultation to the final reveal.
