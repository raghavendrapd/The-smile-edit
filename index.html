Meet Your Smile Architect

Wg Cdr (Dr) Amrita Singh

University Gold Medalist  ·  Army College of Dental Sciences  ·  Founder & Chief Dental Officer

Wg Cdr (Dr) Amrita Singh is an alumnus of the Army College of Dental Sciences and a University Gold Medalist, graduating with a Bachelor of Dental Surgery (BDS) in 2006. A highly decorated officer, she has been honoured with commendations from the Chief of Air Staff and the General Officer Commanding-in-Chief, Eastern Army Command, recognising her consistent pursuit of excellence.

She further expanded her academic and management expertise with an MBA from NMIMS (Narsee Monjee Institute of Management Studies) and an Executive Diploma in Hospital Administration from the Indian School of Business (ISB). Her advanced clinical training includes a Fellowship in Advanced Aesthetic Dentistry from ILAMED, certification as a Soft Tissue Laser Specialist, certification in Digital Smile Design, and a Postgraduate Diploma in Advanced Oral Implantology from Manipal College of Dental Sciences, where she graduated as the course topper.

During her service, she commanded multiple Air Force Dental Centres and trained within one of the most disciplined clinical environments—where meticulous sterilisation protocols, precise diagnosis, and evidence-based treatment approaches are paramount.

As the Founder and Chief Dental Officer of The Smile Edit, she continues to build on this strong foundation, leading the clinic with an unwavering commitment to patient trust, transparency, and ethical dentistry.

Smile Makeover Menu

Treatments Crafted for You

Every treatment is tailored to your goals — because no two smiles, or people, are the same.

Veneers / Smile Design

Enhance your smile with custom-designed veneers that correct stains, gaps, chips, and uneven teeth. Our digital smile design approach ensures a natural, harmonious smile tailored to your facial features.

Root Canal Treatment / Biomimetic Fillings

Root canal treatment removes infected pulp and preserves your natural tooth while eliminating pain. We restore the tooth using biomimetic fillings that mimic the strength and function of natural tooth structure.

Dental Implants / Crowns & Bridges

Dental implants replace missing teeth with natural-looking solutions. Crowns, bridges, and full mouth rehabilitation help rebuild damaged or missing teeth for a healthy, confident smile.

Teeth Whitening / Tooth Jewellery

Professional teeth whitening safely brightens your smile by removing stains and discoloration. Tooth jewellery adds a subtle sparkle to your smile with safe, cosmetic dental adornments.

Complete / Flexible Dentures

Complete and flexible dentures restore comfort, chewing ability, and facial support. Our dentures are custom-crafted for a natural look, secure fit, and improved confidence.

Orthodontic Treatment / Clear Aligners

Orthodontic treatment corrects misaligned teeth and bite problems to improve oral health. Clear aligners offer a nearly invisible and comfortable way to straighten teeth.

Tooth Extraction / Wisdom Tooth Removal

We perform gentle, minimally invasive tooth extractions to relieve pain. Wisdom tooth removal is carried out with precision and care to ensure a smooth and comfortable recovery.

Pediatric Dentistry

Our pediatric dental care focuses on creating a positive and comfortable experience for children. From preventive care to early treatments, we help build healthy dental habits.
